About Me

"Hello, I'm Dr. Tamara Babayeva, a seasoned pediatrician at 78 years.
With a career spanning four decades, I've dedicated 30 years to serving the community at Clalit HMO's community clinic, nurturing and caring for countless families.
As a proud mother of three wonderful daughters, a grandmother to 13 delightful grandchildren, and a great-grandmother to 8 precious little ones, family is my greatest joy and source of pride.
Outside of my professional life, I find immense pleasure in the simple joys of reading, writing, and immersing myself in the beauty of music.
My interests extend beyond the medical field, as I'm deeply engaged in both local and Russian politics.
With each passing year, I cherish the memories of the past and embrace the journey ahead, grateful for the love and experiences that have shaped me into the woman I am today."
